Egg Salad Sandwich


Ingredients:

  • 2 Slices of Wide-Pan Wheat Bread
  • 1 Boiled Egg
  • 1/3 Cup of Greek Yogurt
  • 1 Palmful of Paprika
  • 1 Tsp of Deli Mustard

Steps:

  1. Put bread in the toaster
  2. Slice up boiled eggs and put them in a mixing bowl
  3. Add greek yogurt, paprika, and mustard to the bowl
  4. Mix the ingredients until the yolk is fully mixed
  5. Apply to toasted bread and serve
